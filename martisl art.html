Our school has been providing quality education for over 50 years.Our school focuses mainly in:

Physical Conditioning:

1.Strength Training:

Exercises to build muscle strength, endurance, and power. This may include weight lifting, resistance training, and bodyweight exercises.

2.Basic Techniques:

Fundamental movements and stances specific to the martial art, such as punches, kicks, blocks, and footwork. For example, in Karate, students learn kata (forms) and basic strikes.

3.Full-Contact Sparring:

Engaging in sparring with full protective gear, where practitioners test their skills against resisting opponents. This is usually introduced gradually and with appropriate safety measures.
