Types of Weapon Training:

1.Firearms Training

Focuses on the safe handling, operation, and marksmanship of guns. Includes both live-fire practice and dry-fire drills.

2.Bladed Weapon Training:

Includes techniques for handling knives, swords, and other edged weapons. Training often emphasizes both offensive and defensive maneuvers.

3.Self-Defense Training:

Focuses on using weapons like pepper spray or stun guns in self-defense scenarios..
